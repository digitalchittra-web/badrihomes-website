Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our homestay, booking, rooms, and your stay.

You can book directly on our booking page. Simply fill in your details, select your room and dates, choose your payment method, and submit. We'll confirm your reservation within a few hours via email or WhatsApp.

We offer two flexible payment options:

Payment on Arrival: Pay in cash (NPR), eSewa, Khalti, or IME Pay when you arrive.

Pre-payment: Bank transfer, eSewa, or Khalti before arrival.

Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking up to 48 hours before check-in without any penalty. For cancellations within 48 hours, 50% of the booking amount will be retained. Contact us at homesbadri@gmail.com or WhatsApp for changes.

You'll receive a confirmation email and WhatsApp message with your booking details, payment instructions (if applicable), and check-in information within a few hours of submitting your booking form.

Badri Homes is only a 5-minute walk from Patan Durbar Square, one of Nepal's UNESCO World Heritage Sites. Perfect for exploring the historic temple complex and surrounding markets.

From Tribhuvan International Airport in Kathmandu (about 20 km away):

By Taxi: ~45-60 minutes, approximately NPR 600-800

By Local Bus: ~1.5 hours, inexpensive but slower

By Uber/Ride-hailing: Available from the airport

We can help arrange pickup on request.

Nearby attractions include:

• Patan Durbar Square (5 min walk) - UNESCO World Heritage Site

• Krishna Mandir Temple (2 min walk)

• Newari traditional shops and restaurants

• Local markets for authentic Nepalese crafts

• Astamangal Stupa (15 min walk)

• Machhindranath Temple (walking distance)

Yes, Patan is a safe, tourist-friendly area with a strong community presence. The neighborhood around Patan Durbar Square is well-lit, populated, and secure.
