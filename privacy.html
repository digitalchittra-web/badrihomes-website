Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Badri Homes ("we", "us") operates badrihomes.com. This page explains what information we collect when you visit our website or send us a booking enquiry, and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Booking enquiries: when you submit our enquiry form, we receive the details you provide — your name, email address, phone number, travel dates, room preference, and any message you include.
  • Direct contact: if you reach us by phone, WhatsApp, Viber, or email, we receive the contact details and messages you share with us.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiry and confirm or manage your reservation.
  • To contact you about your stay (for example, check-in arrangements or directions).
  • To meet legal requirements that apply to accommodation providers in Nepal, such as guest registration.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for marketing without your consent.

Third-party services

  • Booking platforms: if you book through Booking.com or Airbnb, your booking data is handled under their own privacy policies.
  • Website services: our site uses Google Fonts, Google Maps, and Firebase (Google) to display content and store enquiry submissions. These services may process technical data such as your IP address.

How long we keep your data

We keep enquiry and booking details only as long as needed to manage your stay and meet our record-keeping obligations, after which they are deleted.

Your choices

You can ask us at any time to see, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you. Just contact us using the details below.
