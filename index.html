About Badri Homes

Studio Apartments
in the Heart of Patan

Named after our late grandfather Badri — the visionary behind this foundation. Founded in 2023, Badri Homes is a new studio apartment committed to making your stay delightful.

Nestled in the heart of Patan, Nepal, each studio is built with top-notch materials to provide utmost comfort. Enjoy a breathtaking view of a vibrant community area filled with temples — a genuine glimpse into the daily life of the Nepalese in Patan.

Just a 2-minute walk from the famous Patan Durbar Square. Bhatbhateni Supermarket is a 15–17 min walk, Labim Mall 17–18 min, and Jhamsikhel only a 5-minute drive away.

Patan's Living Heritage
— on Your Doorstep

The world's finest concentration of medieval art and architecture starts 5 minutes from our front door.

UNESCO Heritage

Patan Durbar Square

One of Asia's finest medieval city squares — temples, courtyards and palaces built by the Malla kings over 1,000 years.

5-minute walk from Badri Homes
Hindu Temple

Krishna Mandir

A stunning 17th-century stone shikhara temple dedicated to Lord Krishna — the architectural centrepiece of Durbar Square.

6-minute walk
Living Culture

Machindranath Jatra

The world's longest chariot festival — a living tradition celebrated right through Patan's streets for over a month each year.

Local Picks

Hidden Gems of Patan

Evening at Durbar Square

After tourists leave at dusk, the square transforms. Locals gather, vendors set up, and the temples glow gold. Ask us for the best spot.

Festival Streets

Patan's festivals are lived, not performed. We'll tell you exactly when and where to go to experience them like a local.

Artisan Workshops

Watch bronze sculptors and thangka painters at work in centuries-old workshops tucked in narrow lanes only locals know.

Real Newari Food

We'll point you to the tiny local eateries serving yomari, bara, and chatamari that no guidebook has found yet.
